Information We Collect

We receive the following information when you install our app and using our service.

  • Located Country, we require the read phone state permission to read your Mobile Country Code (MCC), MCC help us to automatically set up your default country.
  • Log information, we will write to file about how you interact with our service, how the application process and crash information. This log file will remain on your phone and will be sent to us if you want to file a report to us about your issue. File report is in the setting menu. The application will automatically generate email report and attach the log to it. You have a control to remove log attachment.
  • Barcode or QRCode, The Barcode or QRcode that you scanned will remain on your phone without being sent to any server. You have a control for delete it.
  • Screenshots, You able to provide us screenshots to report your issue.
  • Alarm, All the alarm information that you set up will be collected, except label information.
  • Holiday, holiday that you added will be collected.
  • Email, you need provide your email address to us if you want to report your issue.
  • Third-Party Providers, third-Party for help us to report a crash and analytic, such as Firebase. This third party will provide some information about you such as your device information. This information will send to third-party providers server.

  • Barcode or QRCode. We are using this to operate our feature.
  • Alarm information will be collected using analytic, we use this information to improve our app
  • Holiday information from will be collected using analytic, we use this information to improve our app

  • Device Information, We will use this information to investigate your reported issue.
  • Email, We will use the email you provided to communicate back to you about the issue that you reported.
  • Third-Party Providers, We will use information from them to investigate the issue and improve our service.
